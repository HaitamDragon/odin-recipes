Smash Burger

Description

Smash Burger is a simple well known burger containing 2 meat patties, cheese, caramilized onions, and the special sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 meat patties
  • 2 slice of cheese
  • Worcestershire sauce (or alternatively, soy sauce mixed with a sugar sauce)
  • A1 sauce
  • Mayonnaise
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • 2 pickles
  • Pepper
  • Paprika seasoning
  • 2 onions
  • 2 Burger Buns

Steps

    Setup

  1. Take an iron pan and heat it up.
  2. Take any meat, cut 2 pieces and, turn it into a round shape.
  3. Cut your onions into dices.
  4. Cut your pickles into very tiny pieces.
  5. Grab a bowl, add the Worcestershire sauce and the A1 sauce and, mix.

    6. Patty

  6. Put your 2 round meats and, squeeze them until they become a patty.
  7. Add some pepper and salt.
  8. Cook for 2-3 min, then flip it.
  9. Add the mixed Worcestershire + A1 sauce on top of the two patties.
  10. Add a slice of chesse on each patty.
  11. Cook for 2-3 min.

    12. Sauce

  12. Grab a bowl, and add the sliced pickles.
  13. 2tsp of mayonnaise.
  14. 1tsp of mustard.
  15. 2tsp of ketchup.
  16. 1tsp of Worcestershire sauce.
  17. 1tsp of paprika seasoning
  18. Mix together.

    19. Caramilized Onions

  19. Add some oil into an iron pan
  20. Add diced onions.
  21. Cook for atleast 25-30 min.

    22. Making the Burger

  22. Start with a burger bun.
  23. Add 2 cooked meat patties with the cheese on top of them.
  24. Add caramilized onions.
  25. Add the sauce on top.
  26. Add another burger bun.
